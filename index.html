About the Project

A redesign of the Department of the Interior government website. Their mission is protect and manage the Nation’s natural resources and cultural heritage; provide scientific and other information about those resources; and honors its trust responsibilities or special commitments to American Indians, Alaska Natives, and affiliated Island Communities.

Style Guide/First Iterations

As this government department strongly promotes the betterment of Earth and Cultures, we decided to have a more Earthy color scheme, but also maintaining the professionalism of the site.

We decided to simplify the header by removing a category and including it in the About section. The footer was organized alphabetically and we included more detailed information as to how to contact the Department of the interior.
