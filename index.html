Department of the Interior Redesign

Role: UX Designer/Researcher

A redesign of the Department of the Interior government website. Their mission is protect and manage the Nation’s natural resources and cultural heritage; provide scientific and other information about those resources; and honors its trust responsibilities or special commitments to American Indians, Alaska Natives, and affiliated Island Communities.

NAMI

Role: UX Designer/Researcher

NAMI advocates for better mental health awareness for those that deal with mental illness or know someone that is directly impacted by mental health. This redesign was to better help organize site information for the user all while keeping the calming mood.

DUOQ Gaming App

Role: UX Designer/Researcher

"Don’t take the L!” DuoQ is an interactive platonic matchmaking app that provides the user the ability to research and choose a gaming partner to be their DuoQ in any game of their choice. DuoQ allows the user to have an online gaming experience or seek out local players to create tabletop gaming sessions.
